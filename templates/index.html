Abstract

This project harnesses the capabilities of OpenCV to predict the freshness of fruits based on their visual attributes. By employing advanced image processing techniques, the system analyzes key features such as color, texture, and shape to determine the freshness level of various fruits. Utilizing a trained machine learning model, the project can classify the fruits into different freshness categories with high accuracy. This application demonstrates the practical use of computer vision in the food industry, providing a valuable tool for quality control and inventory management. The integration of OpenCV with machine learning algorithms highlights the potential for innovative solutions in ensuring food safety and reducing waste.
